About Us

Rayyan Advocates and Legal Consultants began its operations in Dubai as a full service law firm in the year 2006 and has attained great heights under the patronage of its founder Mr. Salem Al Hammadi.



We have expanded our reach to the Middle East region and are an international oraginsation of highly trained associates who believe in professional integrity.

Our experienced Legal Consultant team provides expertise in various aspects of law, offering a wide array of services and skills to a diverse clientele. With a unique understanding of complex legal requirements, they deliver a proper case assessment, notify of the probable outcome, within a given timeframe. Our satisfied client base include startups who need advice on local laws to large MNC's and government departments.




As one of the leading, trusted local firms with rich experience in law in UAE and GCC, we have represented individual and business customers in Trial and Appellate courts; our expertise has immensely benefited our clients who have tremendous faith in our services. Our firm ensures that businesses are protected by our fair counseling, right from the start.

Our strength is to provide innovative and aggressive solutions with unparalled standards thus ensuring that problems are resolved through our professional and legal means.

Our mission is to provide high quality, dedicated and committed services to our valued client base.
