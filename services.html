Arbitration

As court cases mount leading to pressure on courts and so to save the hardships of litigants, the court encourages arbitration which is an alternate dispute resolution process. It is a settlement of disputes between two parties by an impartial third party and the decision of such third party is binding on such contending parties. The parties though have few grounds to challenge such decision.


Arbitration may be conducted under rules set out by the contending parties and/or arbitrator(s) appointed by them, or may be conducted under the auspices of one of the arbitral institutions such as the Dubai International Arbitration Centre of Commerce (DIAC).


The rules adopted by the contending parties will become the arbitration agreement which confers powers on the arbitrator(s), subject to compliance of certain mandatory provisions of the Arbitration Act.


We at Rayyan Advocates are able to provide the services of an accredited arbitrator to act for you in the dispute and to arrange expert witnesses to provide evidence to an arbitrator.
